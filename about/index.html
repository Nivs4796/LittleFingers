About LittleFingers

LittleFingers was built for parents who wanted a safe digital space where little ones could tap, smash and explore freely — without the worry of accidentally closing important work or changing files. Open LittleFingers, go fullscreen, and let little hands play while the rest of the computer stays safely out of reach.

LittleFingers recently went viral among parents on Instagram, Facebook and TikTok, with millions of views.

Privacy

LittleFingers stays anonymous with no account, no user IDs, no fingerprinting, and analytics limited to GA4 aggregate metrics.
